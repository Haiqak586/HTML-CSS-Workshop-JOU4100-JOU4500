About me

My name is Haiqa Khattak. I live in Ottawa, Canada. I am studying in Digital Journalsim program at the University of Ottawa. I am originally from Pakistan. I came to Canada in 2015. I studied in Journalism Program at algonquin College and continued it at University of Ottawa. I want to become confident and open to learn new things whcih I am having a hard time to do at this point in life. After coming to Canada, I didn't attend school, and was not very familiar with the kind of education here. I think that now I am leaning to adjust to the different education style, but I still am not confident in speaking and about sharing my opinions.

I want to be an opinionated person and share my opinions on public platforms such as Twitter. I aspire to become a journalist to serve my country because I think there is a need for a person who can speak up for the injustices of the society and not only speak out the right from wrong, but also seek justice.
